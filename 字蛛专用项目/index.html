你知道中国的茶杯上为什么要绘上花吗？

天上下雨，滋润泥土，花草丛生，生命得 以延续、成长。茶杯盛着水，我们喝下 去，像花一样得到滋润和灌溉，人类得以 繁衍不息。

Do you know why China's cup is dec- orated with ﬂowers？

Rain falls from the sky, moistening the soil, ﬂowers and plants spur from the earth, life procreates and grows. Cup carries water, and when we drink it, we are irrigated like a ﬂower, and life thus continues.
